Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 mins

Servings: 8 slices

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add water, oil, and vanilla to the bowl, and stir well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until done.
